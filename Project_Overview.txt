
===============================================
AgentOS - Project Overview & Initial Vision
===============================================

This document outlines the **high-level goals**, **architecture decisions**, and **design principles** 
behind the AgentOS frontend development.

------------------------------------------------------

## **Project Goals**
- Develop a **production-ready frontend** for AgentOS.
- Ensure a **minimalist, world-class UI** inspired by Notion, GitKraken, and Google.
- Implement **React.js with modular, scalable architecture**.
- Provide **admin and customer interfaces** with a seamless experience.
